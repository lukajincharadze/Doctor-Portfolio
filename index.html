About Dr. Ekaterina Dzhincharadze

I have worked in public and private medical institutions, including outpatient and inpatient facilities. My scientific and practical focus has been on borderline mental disorders, psychosomatic disorders and stress disorders.

I apply the latest scientific developments and treatment methods, prioritizing empathy, careful attention to patients, openness, tolerance, and confidentiality.

My medical philosophy emphasizes professionalism, responsibility, charisma, kindness, and sinIn my work, I utilize the latest scientific developments and the most modern treatment methods.

The main principle of humanism has been and remains the principle of respectful attitude towards a person - the highest value in the world of living beings."
