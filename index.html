About Dr. Ekaterina Dzhincharadze

I have worked in public and private medical institutions in Moscow, including outpatient and inpatient facilities. My scientific and practical focus has been on borderline neuropsychological disorders and relieving chronic stress factors.

I apply the latest scientific developments and treatment methods, prioritizing empathy, careful attention to patients, collegiality, openness, tolerance, and confidentiality.

My medical philosophy emphasizes professionalism, responsibility, charisma, kindness, and sincere engagement with patients, respecting the highest human values in healthcare.
